Campus Recruitment Training-2125

objective

The primary objective of the CRT program is to enhance the employability of students by providing them with the necessary skills and knowledge required to succeed in the recruitment process.

Curriculum

It typically includes modules on aptitude and technical skills, communication skills, interview techniques, group discussions, resume writing, and personality development.

Faculty and Trainers

The program is facilitated by experienced faculty members, industry experts, and professional trainers with expertise in different domains.

Placement Assistance

The CRT program provides comprehensive placement assistance to students, facilitating interactions between students and potential employers.
The CRT program empowers students to embark on successful careers.
